• Northeastern University Child Health Labs

    We are a group of laboratories at Northeastern University with diverse interests related to child health and development, from studying how children with cerebral palsy learn new skills in virtual environments to understanding how infant sucking patterns can predict future motor skills. We work with children and youth aged 0 to 18 years. We would love to have your child participate in one of our studies!
